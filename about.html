ABOUT US



AGENT | CONNECT was launched to help buyers and sellers of Real Estate identify the best agent to guide them through the buying and/or selling process.

Not all real estate agents have what it takes to be exceptional advocates for their clients.

A large number of Agents are part-time:

Here’s the deal, part time Agents lack the time and knowledge to ensure a successful transaction.

Most Agents live closing-to-closing:

Not all agents have THE business or financial expertise to successfully maneuver through a real estate deal. Once they receive a commission check funds are rarely set aside for marketing, taxes, or savings. You do not want to use an Agent who is under financial pressure, otherwise you could be rushed to close and then left wide open for risk.

80-20 Rule:

It’s true, about 20% of agent’s close 80% of all transactions, and that means most agents close very few deals. You want an experienced agent and one who knows the best way for you to get to the closing table. Experienced agents know the pitfalls, cover ups, and negotiation tactics to ensure you have the best transaction possible.

The AC team used its extensive Real Estate knowledge to create Agent Connect. Agent Connect doesn’t rely solely on technology to match you with the best agent, because we know the best way to find the perfect agent for you is through human intervention.

How does it work? We analyze public reviews, social media postings, agent statistics, market statistics, public records, and brokerage history. Our goal is to match you with a successful agent you will enjoy working with and one who will guide you through the home buying and/or selling process effortlessly and efficiently. Our approach will save you time, money, and future headaches.
